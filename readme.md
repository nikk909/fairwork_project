Principle 1: Fair Pay
1.1 - Ensures workers earn at least the local minimum wage after costs (one point)
Platform workers often have substantial work-related costs to cover, such as transport between jobs, supplies, or fuel, insurance, and maintenance on a vehicle.[1] Workers’ costs sometimes mean their take-home earnings may fall below the local minimum wage.[2] Workers also absorb the costs of extra time commitment, when they spend time waiting or travelling between jobs, or other unpaid activities necessary for their work, such as mandatory training, which are also considered active hours.[3] To achieve this point platforms must ensure that work-related costs do not push workers below local minimum wage. 

The platform takes appropriate steps to ensure both of the following: 

Payment must be on time and in-full.
Workers earn at least the local minimum wage, or the wage set by collective sectoral agreement (whichever is higher) in the place where they work, in their active hours, after costs.


1.2 - Ensures workers earn at least a local living wage after costs (one additional point)
In some places, the minimum wage is not enough to allow workers to afford a basic but decent standard of living. To achieve this point platforms must ensure that work-related costs do not push workers below local living wage. The platform takes appropriate steps to ensure the following: 

Workers earn at least a local living wage, or the wage set by collective sectoral agreement (whichever is higher) in the place where they work, in their active hours, after costs

原则 1：公平薪酬（Fair Pay）

1.1 — 确保工人在扣除成本后至少获得当地最低工资（1 分）

平台工人往往要承担大量与工作相关的成本，例如在不同工作之间往返的交通费、耗材，或车辆的燃油、保险和保养费用。[1] 这些成本有时会使工人的实际到手收入低于当地最低工资。[2] 工人还要承担额外时间成本——例如等待、在工单之间赶路，或其他为完成工作所必需的未付费活动（如强制性培训），这些也都算作“活跃工时”。[3] 要获得这一点，平台必须确保工作相关成本不会使工人收入在扣除成本后低于当地最低工资。

平台需采取适当措施，确保以下两项均成立：

薪酬必须按时、足额支付。
工人在其活跃工时内、扣除成本后，至少能获得其工作所在地的最低工资，或集体行业协议规定的工资（以较高者为准）。
1.2 — 确保工人在扣除成本后至少获得当地生活工资（额外 1 分）

在某些地区，最低工资不足以让工人维持基本但体面的生活水平。要获得这一点，平台必须确保工作相关成本不会使工人收入在扣除成本后低于当地生活工资。平台需采取适当措施，确保：

工人在其活跃工时内、扣除成本后，至少能获得其工作所在地的生活工资，或集体行业协议规定的工资（以较高者为准）。
