# MSK 6 – Research Project: Fairwork Pilots — Assessment Brief & Marking Rubric

**Assessment: Final Presentation (100%)**

---

## Module Overview / 模块概览

**EN**  
In this module you design and complete a small empirical research project on fairness in the platform/gig economy, using the Fairwork principles (Fair Pay, Fair Conditions, Fair Contracts, Fair Management, Fair Representation) as your main analytical lens.

You are supported through weekly workshops and lectures, a 1‑page mini‑proposal, supervision meetings/ongoing feedback as requested, progress reflection sheets, dedicated deep‑work sessions in class, and three formative checkpoints (proposal, plan presentation, draft results) that prepare you directly for the graded task.

In MSK 6 you demonstrate your learning through a final presentation (100%) that shows your ability to communicate your research and findings in a concise, engaging, visual, and audience‑friendly way. Your talk should highlight your key question, methods, and findings, and explain why they matter for Fairwork in practice in conversational and engaging language.

**中文**  
本模块要求你围绕平台/零工经济中的公平性，设计并完成一项小型实证研究，以 Fairwork 原则（公平薪酬、公平条件、公平合同、公平管理、公平代表）作为主要分析视角。

课程支持包括：每周工作坊与讲座、一页迷你提案、按需督导/持续反馈、进度反思表、课堂深度工作时段，以及三个形成性检查点（提案、计划展示、结果草稿），直接为最终评分任务做准备。

在 MSK 6 中，你通过最终演示（占成绩 100%）展示学习成果：需以简洁、有吸引力、可视化、面向听众的方式呈现研究与发现。演讲应突出核心问题、方法与发现，并用口语化、易于理解的语言说明其对 Fairwork 实践的意义。

---

## Final Presentation – Task Description / 最终演示任务说明

**EN**  
You will give a short presentation (approx. 10–15 minutes) covering:

- **A. Title, Topic & Context** — Who/what your project is about, and 1–2 sentences on why this matters in the platform/gig economy.
- **B. Research Question & Fairwork Principle** — One clear research question, and how it links to at least one Fairwork principle.
- **C. Method & Data** — What you did, with whom/what, and why this was feasible and ethical in one semester.
- **D. Key Findings with Evidence** — 2–4 key findings supported by simple evidence (e.g. quotes, tables, graphs).
- **E. Reflection & Next Steps** — Your contribution to understanding fairness in this case, main limitations, and ideas for future research or practice.

You must follow XU's academic integrity rules (proper citation, no plagiarism, correct use of AI according to university guidelines), and Fairwork ethical standards for research with workers and sensitive topics.

**中文**  
你需进行约 10–15 分钟的短演示，涵盖：

- **A. 标题、主题与背景** — 项目研究对象是谁/什么，并用 1–2 句说明其在平台/零工经济中为何重要。
- **B. 研究问题与 Fairwork 原则** — 一个清晰的研究问题，及其与至少一条 Fairwork 原则的关联。
- **C. 方法与数据** — 做了什么、与谁/用什么材料，以及为何在一学期内可行且符合伦理。
- **D. 关键发现与证据** — 2–4 条关键发现，辅以简单证据（如引文、表格、图表）。
- **E. 反思与下一步** — 对本案例中公平性理解的贡献、主要局限，以及对未来研究或实践的想法。

须遵守 XU 学术诚信规定（正确引用、禁止抄袭、按学校指南正确使用 AI），以及 Fairwork 针对劳动者与敏感议题研究的伦理标准。

**你如何清晰讲述实际做了什么，以及为何可行且符合伦理。**

| ---: | --- | --- |
| **0 – Fail** | The audience cannot tell what method you used or how you collected data; ethical issues are ignored; the project appears clearly unrealistic for one semester. | 听众无法判断所用方法或如何收集数据；忽视伦理；项目明显不适合一学期完成。 |
